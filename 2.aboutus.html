Introduction to Cress Jane Hospital

Cress Jane Hospital, located in the vibrant city of Colombo, Sri Lanka, stands as a cornerstone of advanced healthcare and compassionate patient care. Founded in 2005, this premier medical institution has evolved from a modest clinic into a leading hospital renowned for its excellence in various medical disciplines. Since its inception, Cress Jane Hospital has marked significant milestones in its journey. Initially established to address the growing healthcare needs of the community, it has steadily expanded its services and capabilities. Over the years, the hospital has introduced cutting-edge medical technologies, established specialized care units, and expanded its network of expert physicians. These advancements have positioned Cress Jane Hospital as a beacon of healthcare innovation and quality in Sri Lanka. Our growth reflects our unwavering commitment to enhancing patient outcomes and accessibility to high-quality care. Today, we continue to build on our legacy of excellence, with a focus on integrating modern medical practices, advancing research, and providing compassionate care to every patient who walks through our doors.

Mission Statement

At Cress Jane Hospital, our mission is to provide compassionate and comprehensive healthcare services that meet the highest standards of excellence. We are dedicated to delivering patient-centered care through a seamless integration of advanced medical technologies, highly skilled professionals, and a supportive environment. Our goal is to enhance the well-being of our patients and their families by offering personalized treatment plans, fostering a culture of respect and empathy, and continually advancing our practices to address the evolving needs of our community.

Vision Statement

Our vision at Cress Jane Hospital is to become a beacon of healthcare excellence and innovation in Sri Lanka, renowned for setting the highest standards in patient care and medical practice. We aim to expand our reach and impact by continuously improving our facilities, embracing cutting-edge technologies, and cultivating a team of exceptional healthcare professionals. Our aspiration is to lead in both medical advancements and patient satisfaction, creating healthier communities and making a positive difference in the lives of individuals across the nation. Through unwavering commitment to quality, compassion, and progress, we seek to build a healthier future for all.
